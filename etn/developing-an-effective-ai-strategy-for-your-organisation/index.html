Developing an Effective AI strategy for your organisation

Are you ready to take your AI strategy to the next level?

Join Intelliverse AI on July 28th for a live event on how to develop an effective AI strategy for your organization. This event will feature expert speakers from leading AI companies, who will share their insights on the latest trends in AI, how to assess your organization’s AI readiness, and how to build a successful AI strategy.

Topics to be covered:

  • The latest trends in AI, including natural language processing, machine learning, and deep learning
  • How to assess your organization’s AI readiness
  • How to build a successful AI strategy
  • Case studies from leading AI companies

  • John Wafula Senior AI Engineer AICE
  • Tony Boobier AI expert, Author
  • Chad Assoulama (Mara)
  • Ibrahim Ajiboye (Data Analyst and BI expert ProForecast Ltd)

  • Get insights from expert speakers on how to build a successful AI strategy

Emerging trends in AI:

  • Natural language processing (NLP)is becoming increasingly sophisticated, allowing machines to understand and respond to human language in a more natural way.
  • Machine learning (ML)is being used to solve a wide range of problems, from fraud detection to customer service.
  • Deep learningis a subset of ML that uses artificial neural networks to learn from data. Deep learning is being used to power some of the most impressive AI applications today, such as self-driving cars and facial recognition.
